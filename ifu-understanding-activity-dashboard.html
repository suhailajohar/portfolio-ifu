Understanding Your Activity Dashboard

Wellness Activity Monitor

1. Purpose

This document explains how to review and interpret weekly activity data displayed in the companion mobile application dashboard.

2. Overview

The activity dashboard summarizes recorded physical activity data collected by the Wellness Activity Monitor. The dashboard is designed to support user awareness of personal activity patterns over time.

3. Weekly Activity Summary

The dashboard provides a weekly summary of recorded activity, including:

  • Total step count
  • Active minutes
  • Estimated calorie expenditure

Weekly summaries allow users to observe trends rather than focusing on single-day values.

4. Interpreting Activity Levels

General physical activity guidance published by the World Health Organization (WHO) recommends that adults engage in regular physical activity as part of a healthy lifestyle.

The activity dashboard presents recorded data for informational purposes only. Users are encouraged to interpret trends in the context of their personal goals and daily routines.

CAUTION
Activity data displayed in the dashboard is informational and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice or clinical assessment.

5. Understanding Weekly Trends

Weekly trend views illustrate changes in activity levels over time. Viewing data across multiple weeks may help users identify consistent patterns, such as increased or decreased activity during specific periods.

Short-term fluctuations are normal and may occur due to changes in routine, device wear time, or daily activities.

6. Dashboard Indicators

Indicator Description
Daily Average Represents the average value calculated from recorded daily activity within a selected week
Weekly Total Represents the cumulative activity recorded across a seven-day period
Trend Arrow Indicates relative increase or decrease compared to the previous week

7. Limitations of the Data

Recorded activity data may be influenced by factors such as device placement, wear duration, and user movement patterns. The dashboard does not account for all forms of physical activity.

8. Additional Information

For further guidance on physical activity, users may refer to publicly available resources provided by recognized health organizations.
